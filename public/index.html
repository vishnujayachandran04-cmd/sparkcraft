Our Services

Structural Welding

FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started with SparkCraft.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes — all quotes are completely free and come with a detailed breakdown. No obligation, no pressure.

How long does a typical project take?

It depends on the scope. Small repairs can be done same-day, while large fabrication jobs may take 1–2 weeks. We always provide a timeline upfront.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. SparkCraft is fully licensed, bonded, and carries comprehensive liability insurance on every job we take.

What types of welding do you offer?

We work with MIG, TIG, Stick (SMAW), and flux-core welding methods — choosing the right one based on material and application.

Can you weld on my job site?

Yes. We have fully-equipped mobile welding trucks and can come directly to construction sites, farms, factories, or your home.

Do you offer a workmanship warranty?

Yes. All our welds and fabricated structures come with a workmanship guarantee. If something's not right, we come back and fix it at no charge.
